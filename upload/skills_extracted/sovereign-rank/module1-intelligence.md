### 2. Keyword Universe
Map at minimum 50 target keywords before launch:
- Head terms (high volume, high competition): list 5
- Mid-tail terms (medium volume, medium competition): list 15
- Long-tail terms (low volume, low competition — WIN HERE FIRST): list 30

For each keyword capture:
- Monthly search volume
- Keyword difficulty score
- Current SERP feature saturation (what features appear)
- Top ranking domain and its DR
- Featured snippet present? (Yes/No)
- PAA questions present? Count them

### 3. Competitor Matrix
For the top 5 competitors:
| Domain | DR | Backlinks | Content Volume | Top Keywords | Weakness |
|--------|-----|-----------|----------------|--------------|---------|
| | | | | | |

**Weakness analysis — look for:**
- Thin content (under 800 words on key pages)
- No schema markup
- Poor Core Web Vitals
- Old content (not updated in 2+ years)
- Missing PAA coverage
- No video content
- No original data/research

### 4. Topical Gap Map
List 10 subtopics within the niche that have:
- Search volume > 500/month
- No single authoritative resource ranking
- PAA questions with weak current answers
These are your first 10 content targets.

### 5. Entity Landscape
Identify the key entities Google associates with this niche:
- People (experts, authors, thought leaders)
- Brands (established companies in the space)
- Concepts (core terms Google has KG entries for)
- Places (if local relevance exists)

Your content must co-cite these entities to establish topical relevance
in Google's Knowledge Graph.

### 6. SERP Feature Analysis
For your top 10 target keywords, record which SERP features appear:
- AI Overview: Yes/No
- Featured Snippet: Yes/No — if Yes, what format (paragraph/list/table)?
- PAA boxes: Yes/No — if Yes, how many?
- Local Pack: Yes/No
- Video Carousel: Yes/No
- Shopping Carousel: Yes/No
- Knowledge Panel: Yes/No

This tells you exactly which modules to prioritize.
High AI Overview presence → prioritize Module 7 (AI Overview citation strategy)
High Local Pack presence → prioritize Module 2 entity + Google Business Profile
High Video Carousel presence → YouTube is mandatory, not optional

### 7. Decision Gate Output
Based on the above, output:
- LAUNCH STRATEGY: (which 3 modules to execute simultaneously at launch)
- FIRST 10 CONTENT TARGETS: (the topical gaps identified in section 4)
- AUTHORITY BENCHMARK: (the DR of the weakest competitor on page 1 = your near-term target)
- TIMELINE ESTIMATE: (conservative months to reach page 1 for first keyword)
